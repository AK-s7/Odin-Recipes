Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with paneer cubes cooked in a tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes
  • Butter
  • Tomato puree
  • Onion paste
  • Cream
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Heat butter and saut√© ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add onion paste and cook till golden.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add cream and simmer.

Classic Veg Pasta

A simple and tasty vegetable pasta made with fresh veggies, garlic, and tomato sauce. Perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Capsicum
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • BlackPepper
  • Chili Flakes

  1. Boil pasta until soft and drain.
  2. Heat oil, add garlic and onion.

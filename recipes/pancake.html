Pancake

Soft and fluffy pancakes served with syrup or fruits, perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, mix well.
  3. Heat pan and add butter.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook till golden.
